Job/Career Path Recommendation System

Our intelligent system matches job seekers with opportunities using advanced skills extraction (NLP), experience analysis, and collaborative filtering. We provide personalized job recommendations by analyzing your resume or CV and matching your skills with job descriptions using artificial intelligence. The system identifies skill gaps and recommends relevant courses to help you advance your career. Additionally, we offer career growth suggestions with visual career path progression based on your current role and skills. This comprehensive approach reduces the time spent searching for jobs and helps you understand what skills you need to develop for your next career step. Simply upload your resume or CV, and our program will provide jobs that match your skills, courses to fill skill gaps, and a clear career progression path.
